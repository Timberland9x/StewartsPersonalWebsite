Relevance Weighted Multiple Meeting Scheduler

Project Description: Developed a tool that allows organizers to prioritize attendees based on relevance, and attendees to select availability in increments of .25. The tool aims to improve the scheduling process, providing a weighted average schedule that considers availability and relevance, resulting in more efficient and effective planning of events, conferences, and meetings.
